Whistler Ski + Snowboard Festival

Frequently asked questions

Q: What dates does the Whistler Ski & Snowboard Festival take place?
The Whistler Ski & Snowboard Festival typically takes place in April, but the exact dates can vary each year. Be sure to check our official website or social media for up-to-date information on this year's event schedule.
Q: Where is the festival held?
The festival is hosted at Whistler Blackcomb, located in Whistler, British Columbia, Canada. This iconic resort offers world-class skiing and snowboarding across its vast terrain, with events held throughout the village and mountain areas.
Q: Can I buy tickets for individual events?
Yes, tickets for individual events are available for purchase, including concerts, competitions, and après-ski activities. For all-access passes or multi-event tickets, please refer to our ticketing page for more details.
Q: Do I need to be an experienced skier or snowboarder to attend the festival?
No! The Whistler Ski & Snowboard Festival welcomes all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, there are activities, lessons, and events for everyone to enjoy. Spectators of all abilities can also enjoy the action-packed competitions and live entertainment.
Q: Are there any family-friendly activities?
Absolutely! The festival offers various family-friendly activities, including kids’ ski lessons, snow play areas, and special events tailored for younger audiences. It's a great experience for families to enjoy together.
Q: What should I wear to the festival?
Dress in layers to stay comfortable while on the slopes or attending outdoor events. Be sure to bring ski or snowboard gear if you're planning to hit the slopes. For après-ski or evening events, bring warm clothing as temperatures can drop in the evening.
Q: Is there parking available at the festival?
Yes, there are several parking lots near the resort. However, parking can fill up quickly during the festival. We recommend using public transportation or taking advantage of shuttle services to avoid the hassle of finding a spot.
Q: Are pets allowed at the festival?
For safety and comfort reasons, pets are not allowed at most festival events. However, certain outdoor areas may allow pets, so please check the festival guidelines for more details.
Q: Are there any discounts available for festival tickets?
We offer discounts for early bird ticket purchases, group bookings, and local residents. Be sure to sign up for our newsletter or check our website for any current promotions or special offers.
Q: Can I rent ski or snowboard equipment at the festival?
Yes, rental equipment is available at Whistler Blackcomb for skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports. You can reserve your gear in advance to ensure availability during the festival.
